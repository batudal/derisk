Introducing De-Risk

Why are we doing this? What problem are we solving? Why now?

Batu Dal - 16 May, 2023

Why am I doing this?

When I was working in the family business, I was responsible for the R&D of a modular construction system.

Scope of my responsibility involved designing the factory line and the construction process, as well as the product itself.

I studied Toyota Production System, Lean Manufacturing, and Six Sigma to implement a production system that would be efficient and effective.

As I moved on to entrepreneurship, I realized that the same principles could be applied to startups(Lean Startup).

I started to look for a tool that would help me implement these principles in my startup, but I couldn't find one.

Many solutions end up with a PDF template that you have to fill out, or a checklist that you have to follow.

What problem am I solving?

I am building a risk management tool for early-stage projects.

It is a tool that helps you identify, analyze, and mitigate risks in your project.

It helps you manage multiple projects with an analytics dashboard to oversee gross risk and costs. Instead of printed templates and checklists, it is a digital tool that helps you manage your projects in a collaborative way.
